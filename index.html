Soumyajit Paul

সৌম্যজিৎ

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Liverpool with Sven Schewe and Dominik Wojtczak .

Previously, I was a Teaching and Research Fellow (ATER) at Université Paris Cité affiliated to IRIF.

I completed my PhD from LaBRI, Université de Bordeaux under the supervision of Hugo Gimbert, B. Srivathsan and Olivier Ly.

Broadly I am interested in algorithmic game theory. In the past, I have worked on complexity of computing equilibrium in games with imperfect information. I have also been working on theoretical models for Bridge Bidding.

My other research interests include formal methods and verification, probabilistic systems, combinatorics. I also enjoy thinking about interesting problems with theoretical flavour that model real life situations.
